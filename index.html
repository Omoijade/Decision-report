Decision Record
No login • Copy / Print • Stays on your device

Capture the decision you just discussed.

A simple shared surface for the last 10 minutes of a meeting: write the decision, the reason, and who does what next—then copy it into your usual message or print/save a PDF for internal records.

Open the tool How to use it (10 min)

Drafts stay on this device (in your browser). Nothing is sent anywhere unless you copy it out. Avoid sensitive personal data.

Example record
DECISION RECORD

Decision: Change weekly handover time to 08:30 (from next Monday)
Ref: CWT-YYYY-MM-DD
Stage: For review
Revisit by: YYYY-MM-DD

Reason
- Current handover overlaps with first appointments.
- Trade-off: slightly earlier start for one team.

Next steps (open)
- Alex: update rota and notify the team by Friday
- Sam: adjust room booking for next week
Shared surface

Write it while everyone is still in the room, so the group aligns on what was actually decided.

Survives turnover

Decisions don’t disappear into memory. New staff can quickly see recent decisions and actions.

Meetings with purpose

Clear outcomes and owners means fewer follow‑ups, less rework, and better coordination.

Use it in the last 10 minutes

Keep it short. The goal is shared clarity—not perfect writing.

  1. 1
    Name the decision
    One sentence is enough.
  2. 2
    Write the reason
    2–4 lines: why now + trade-off + what would make us revisit.
  3. 3
    Assign one next step
    Start with a name: “Alex: …”
  4. 4
    Copy or print
    Paste into your usual message, or print/save a PDF for records.
