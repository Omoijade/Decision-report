Decision Record Open tool

Privacy

This is a simple, static website. It does not require an account.

What data is stored

The tool stores your draft in your browser on this device (localStorage) so you can refresh the page without losing work.

What data is sent

The site does not send your decision content to a server. Your content only leaves this device if you copy/paste it into a message, or print/save it as a PDF.

Sensitive information

Avoid entering sensitive personal data. Use the tool for operational/team decisions and coordination.

Analytics

By default, no analytics are included in these files. If you add analytics later, do not collect decision content.
